Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMJAY empanelment?

PMJAY empanelment is the process through which eligible hospitals are approved to provide treatment under the Ayushman Bharat scheme.

Why do hospitals need a PMJAY consultant?

Hospitals require support because empanelment, package mapping, claims documentation, TMS workflow and audit preparedness require strong operational discipline.

Why are Ayushman Bharat claims rejected?

Common reasons include incomplete documentation, wrong package selection, weak clinical notes, delayed uploads and incomplete discharge summaries.

How can hospitals improve PMJAY profitability?

Hospitals can improve profitability through better package selection, process discipline, reduced claim rejection and strong documentation workflows.

Do you support hospitals in West Bengal?

Yes. The service focuses on hospitals in West Bengal, Bihar and Uttar Pradesh, with office presence in Kolkata.
