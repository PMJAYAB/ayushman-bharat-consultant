Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMJAY empanelment?

PMJAY empanelment is the process through which eligible hospitals are approved to provide treatment under the Ayushman Bharat scheme.

Why do hospitals need a PMJAY consultant?

Hospitals need support because empanelment, package mapping, claims documentation, TMS workflow and audit preparedness require strong operational discipline.

Why are claims rejected?

Common reasons include incomplete documentation, wrong package selection, weak clinical notes, delayed uploads and incomplete discharge summaries.

How can hospitals improve profitability?

Hospitals can improve PMJAY profitability through better package selection, cost discipline, reduced claim rejection and strong documentation workflows.

Do you support West Bengal hospitals?

Yes. The service focuses on hospitals in West Bengal, Bihar and Uttar Pradesh, with office presence in Kolkata.

What is the first step?

The first step is a readiness discussion to understand hospital type, specialties, scheme status, documentation gaps and claim process maturity.
