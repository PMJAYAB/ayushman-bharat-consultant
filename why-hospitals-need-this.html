Why Hospitals Need This

Ayushman Bharat can bring patient volume, but without strong processes it can also create delayed payments, claim deductions, staff confusion and profitability pressure.

Common Mistakes Hospitals Make

Weak Documentation

Clinical notes, investigations, discharge summary and package justification are not aligned before claim submission.

Incorrect Package Mapping

Wrong package selection or poor package-cost understanding can reduce margins or trigger queries.

No Daily Claim Review

Hospitals often review claims only after problems appear, instead of tracking pending, submitted, queried and paid claims daily.

Disconnected Teams

Doctors, nursing, billing, TPA and finance teams work separately, creating gaps between treatment, documentation and claim submission.

Delayed Query Closure

Queries remain unresolved because ownership, escalation and evidence collection are not clearly defined.

No Profitability Dashboard

Hospitals track revenue but not package-wise cost, deductions, payment TAT and cash-flow impact.

What Good Scheme Operations Look Like

  • Clear empanelment roadmap and owner-wise checklist
  • Admission-to-claim workflow defined before launch
  • Daily dashboard for current claims, old pending claims and queries
  • Package-wise margin and deduction visibility
  • Regular training of billing, TPA, clinical and nursing teams
  • Monthly leadership review of revenue, payment delay and leakage
